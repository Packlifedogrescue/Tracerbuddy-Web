----- DESCRIPTION (paste into Description field) -----

Track every shot. Understand every round. Play better golf.

TracerBuddy is the complete performance system for golfers who want to actually improve — not just keep score.

SHOT TRACKING & SCORECARDS
• GPS shot tracking on 42,000+ courses worldwide
• Digital scorecards: stroke play, Stableford, match play, skins & best ball
• Hole-by-hole breakdown with fairways, GIR, and putts
• Shot replay and miss direction tracking

APPLE WATCH — SWINGTRACE
• Automatic swing detection from your wrist
• Swing speed, tempo, and backswing duration every shot
• No manual input needed during your round

AI COACH CARDS
• After every round: your biggest strokes leak identified
• Club misfiring detection and miss pattern analysis
• Custom practice plan from your actual data
• Handicap tracking and goal progression

PERFORMANCE STATS
• Strokes Gained breakdown
• Club Confidence distances from your real swing data
• 3-putt rate, 1-putt rate, GIR %, fairway %

COURSE MAPS & WEATHER
• Satellite maps with live GPS distances
• Wind and weather overlay
• Hazard and layup yardages

SOCIAL & COMPETITION
• Buddy Battles — head-to-head match play
• Group tournaments and leaderboards

Your first round is completely free — no credit card required.
